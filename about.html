about me

hi! i'm luca guerrera. i use they/them pronouns and i study education. i graduated from Wesleyan University in May 2025 with a bachelors in education studies and computer science. in college, i discovered that i am incredibly passionate about seeking equity in education, particularly at the intersection of race and disability. i hope to eventually become a professor and teach education studies (which one might find ironic).

in my spare time, i enjoy printmaking, reading, knitting, and making art. please feel free to reach out to me.
